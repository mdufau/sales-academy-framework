## What is a sales competency framework?

A competency framework defines **what "good" looks like** for each sales role, at each level of seniority. It answers the question:

> *"What does a high-performing [Sales Rep / Account Manager / Sales Manager] at [level X] know, do, and demonstrate?"*

It serves as the backbone for:
- Training and certification paths
- Onboarding programs
- Performance reviews and development plans
- Recruitment criteria
- Coaching conversations
