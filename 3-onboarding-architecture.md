## The 3 dimensions of sales onboarding

Effective sales onboarding addresses three dimensions simultaneously:

| Dimension | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Cultural** | Who we are, what we stand for, how we work |
| **Commercial** | Our products, our customers, our sales process |
| **Operational** | Our tools, our processes, our reporting |

Most onboarding programs over-invest in operational content and under-invest in culture and commercial fundamentals.
